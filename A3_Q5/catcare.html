Tips for taking care of your favourite Cat

Doesn't matter if you are an veteran dog owner or thinking about newly adopting one

Check out these tips for taking care of your pet friend!

1. Human companionship is a must!

2. Make sure your pet gets regular and suitable meals with a constant supply of fresh water

3. Provide a clean and comfy bed

4. Provide outdoor access or be prepared to empty and clean a litter tray on a daily basis

5. Provide it with a stimulating and safe environment

6. Groom it regularly. Longhaired cats require daily grooming

7. Have it neutered between 4 and 6 months old

8. Vaccinate against the major feline diseases regularly

9. Worm regularly and provide treatment for fleas

10. Take the cat to the vet when it shows any sign of illness
